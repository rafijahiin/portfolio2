Officer – Partnership & Innovation
Swisscontact · ASTHA Community Paramedic Project · Nov 2021 – Jan 2025
Partnerships & digital project implementation

Led partnerships, digital innovation, and scaling for ASTHA, linking curriculum, platforms, employers and government so Community Paramedics (CPs) could actually work, earn, and serve people across 12 districts.

  • Strategic partnerships & MoUs: Orchestrated partnerships with Shomvob, Zabai, Zaynax Health and Roche Bangladesh to expand job opportunities, telehealth services, and specialised cancer screening roles for CPs.
  • Digital innovation: Spearheaded migration of the CP curriculum to a mobile-optimised, open-source Moodle LMS for 30 training institutes, integrating interactive H5P and 2D/3D content and developing standards for low-bandwidth modules.
  • Capacity building: Organised a six-day ToT and follow-up support for CP faculty, and integrated entrepreneurship skills so CPs could run sustainable health practices, not just pass exams.
  • Employment & income: Co-designed job fairs and digital job-platform linkages (e.g. with Shomvob) that connected hundreds of CP graduates to employers and contributed to measurable income growth for graduates.
  • Policy & government engagement: Supported national consultative meetings and engagement with DGHS and family planning actors to position CPs in primary health and referral discussions.
  • Service diversification: Helped roll out dengue-response trainings with the CDC unit and pilot cancer screening initiatives with Roche, broadening CP roles in early detection and community awareness.

Evaluation & analytics

Co-leading the analytical backbone of UNFPA Bangladesh’s 10th CPE, from framework design to data synthesis, with structured use of AI tools across the evaluation workflow.

  • Co-designed the evaluation framework, ToC refinements, and full evaluation matrix aligned with UNEG and the UNFPA 2024 Evaluation Handbook.
  • Built and maintained evidence systems: findings grids, indicator trend tables, LNOB trackers, and effectiveness annexes.
  • Led quantitative trend analysis and supported triangulation with BDHS and other national datasets.
  • Planned and supported multi-district field missions, FGDs and KIIs, including tools, sampling logic, and note-taking structures.
  • Introduced AI tools (NotebookLM, Gemini, LLMs) for document review, transcript handling, and consistency checks under ethical safeguards.
